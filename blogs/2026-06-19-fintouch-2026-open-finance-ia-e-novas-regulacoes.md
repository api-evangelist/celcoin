Coverage of Fintouch 2026, Brazil's largest fintech event organized by ABFintechs, where the sector's shift into a maturity phase of market consolidation and stronger regulatory compliance took center stage. Key themes included Open Finance as foundational for SMEs, embedded finance turning retailers into financial-service providers (credit origination, onboarding, KYC, collections), AI-driven hyperpersonalization of credit using Open Finance data, security-by-design as mandatory, and Central Bank emphasis that rigorous regulation induces innovation.
